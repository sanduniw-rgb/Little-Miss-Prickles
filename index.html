Behind the prickles

Hi, I'm Duni, nice to meet you!

A Canberra mum, professional plate-spinner, and the person behind Little Miss Prickles. If you looked up "too much on her plate" in the dictionary, you'd probably find a photo of me looking mildly caffeinated.

I'm a general manager running multiple businesses, a role I was absolutely thrown into, sink or swim style, by a boss who saw something in me before I did. Spoiler: I swam. Mostly.

I'm also the behind-the-scenes engine of my husband's gardening biz (he does the digging, I do the dollars). We've been together since we were 15 (we're 30 now), which means we've done all the growing up, the hard bits, and the good bits side by side. There's genuinely no one else I'd want in my corner for any of it.

Then there's our two-year-old, who has one speed and absolutely no interest in my to-do list. He keeps me present in a way no productivity app ever could. Turns out a toddler who wants to read the same book seventeen times in a row is actually pretty good at teaching you to slow down and appreciate the completely ordinary.

Little Miss Prickles is what happens when someone with too much on their plate also has too many feelings about greeting cards. I've always made cards for people rather than buying them, not because I had to, but because I genuinely believe a card that actually gets the person receiving it hits differently. Something that makes them think "she really knows me" or "that betch." That's the good stuff.

I believe card-giving is one of the most underrated things we do for each other. In a world of quick texts and voice notes, there's something genuinely special about holding something someone made, posted, and thought about, just for you. I want to bring that back. With puns. Obviously.

Every card is designed with too much love and not enough sleep, printed locally here in Canberra, and sent with the hope that it makes someone snort-laugh, ugly cry, or both.

If you've ever wanted to give someone a card that really meant something...
welcome home ♡

Love, Duni

Questions? We've got answers.

If you can't find what you're looking for, just drop us a line at hello@littlemissprickles.com.au

General
What makes LMP different from a regular card shop?

Most card shops give you the same twelve options you've seen a hundred times. A generic birthday cake, a sad balloon, maybe a sunset if you're lucky. LMP makes cards that actually sound like something a real human would say. Cheeky, heartfelt, punny, and always a little bit unexpected. We also post them straight to your door so you don't have to panic in a service station car park on someone's birthday.

Where are you based and do you ship Australia wide?

We're proudly based in Canberra, ACT. We ship cards all over Australia so no matter where your people are, we've got them covered.

How long does delivery take?

We post via Australia Post regular mail which typically takes 2 to 5 business days depending on where you are. For subscription cards we send them out approximately 2 weeks before your date so you have plenty of time to write something lovely (or cheeky) and post it on.

Cards
Are the cards printed locally?

Yes! Every card is printed locally here in Australia. We're big believers in supporting local and keeping things close to home.

What sizes are the cards?

Our cards are standard A5 greeting card size, perfect for popping in a regular envelope and posting to someone you love. Or someone you tolerate. We don't judge.

Can I order a custom card?

Not just yet but it's on the way! Keep an eye on our socials for updates.

Subscription
How does the subscription work?

You pick a plan (Seedling, In Bloom, or Full Bloom), pay, and then fill in a simple form telling us the dates and occasions you want covered throughout the year. We pick the perfect card for each one and post it to your door approximately 2 weeks before the date. All you have to do is write something inside and send it. Easy.

When do I fill in my dates?

Right after you pay! Once your payment goes through you'll be redirected to a short form where you fill in all the details for each card. The date, the occasion, the vibe you're going for, and any little notes that might help us pick the perfect card. Takes about 5 to 10 minutes and then you're sorted for the year.

What if I need to change a date mid-year?

Life happens, we get it. Just drop us an email at hello@littlemissprickles.com.au and we'll update your details. As long as we haven't already packed your card we can make changes no problem.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yep, no lock-in contracts and no guilt trips. You can cancel your subscription anytime. We'll be sad to see you go but we'll respect the decision. (We might send you off with a good pun though. Can't help ourselves.)

What if I have more dates than my plan allows?

You can upgrade your plan anytime to add more card slots.

Do you write in the card or do I?

That one's all you. We pick and post the card and you write the message.

Independent Hearts
How does Independent Hearts work?

Independent Hearts is our program for older Australians in Canberra who want to keep sending cards to the people they love, on their own terms. We sit down together over a phone call or in person, work through all the important dates, and set everything up on your behalf. From there, cards arrive at your door throughout the year ready to sign and send. No apps, no forms, no fuss.

Do I need to use a computer or app?

Not at all. The whole point of Independent Hearts is that you don't need any technology to use it. We handle the setup together over a call or in person and take care of everything from there.

Can my family set it up for me?

Absolutely. Family members can set up and pay for an Independent Hearts subscription as a gift. We'll make sure the whole experience still feels like it belongs to you, because it does.

Is Independent Hearts only available in Canberra?

For now, yes. We're starting local so Duni can meet people in person and do things properly. We'd love to expand this program in the future, so watch this space.

Sorted
How does Sorted work for businesses?

Sorted is LMP's program for businesses who want to make sure every team member gets a card on their birthday. You sign up, give us your team's names and birthdays, tell us the vibe you want (we'd suggest keeping it workplace appropriate but hey, you know your team), and we send the right card to your door before every date throughout the year. Simple, thoughtful, done.

Can I add new staff mid-year?

Yes! People join teams all year round and we've built Sorted with that in mind. Just get in touch when someone new joins and we'll add them to your plan.

What if someone leaves the team?

Just let us know and we'll remove them. No awkward birthday cards for the person who quit in February. We've all been there.

Still have a question?

We'd love to hear from you. Drop us a line and we'll get back to you faster than you can say "I forgot their birthday."
