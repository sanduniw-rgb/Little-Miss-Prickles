Behind the prickles

Hi, I'm the face behind Little Miss Prickles

I started Little Miss Prickles because I've always believed that a handwritten card is one of the most meaningful things you can give someone. In a world of texts and DMs, there's something magical about holding something made just for you.

I'm a Canberra local — mum, business owner, and someone who genuinely loves finding the perfect card. Every design is created with heart, and every subscription is packed with care.

Whether you're after something for a birthday, a new baby, or just a little "I was thinking of you" — I've got you covered.
